About

I'm Abdul Haaris — a UI/UX designer who believes that great design isn't just about looks. It's about creating moments that resonate, interfaces that breathe, and products that genuinely improve how people interact with technology.

Every pixel I place is intentional. Every interaction I design is meaningful. I approach design the way an architect approaches space — with deep respect for the humans who will inhabit it.

My work spans wellness apps, spiritual platforms, and knowledge tools — always with the same philosophy: design should feel inevitable.
